About

Lyra Entertainment presents Azymuth in Tbilisi, Georgia. “Samba Doido” in Portuguese means a crazy, insane samba. So is called the music genre born under the Brazilian sun, played by the legendary Trio Azymuth. This is a synthesis of Northern funk, jazz and Southern Samba Soul, which creates a cosmic psychedelia and becomes a favorite tune (melody) for all generations. Azymuth creates a psychedelic jazz architecture, a cosmic Funk and overwhelming Groove, music without borders, which leads souls to the mystical world through dance moves of the body.
